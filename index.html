Willow House

Willow House is a beautiful Colonial Home located in the quiet northern suburb of Wooloowin. Our home provides accomodation, meals, care & support to both males and females. We provide a home for people requiring care due to mental health issues or for people who just need a caring and supportive environment in which to thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality personal care and accomodation for residents while ensuring their rights,privacy and dignity are respected at all times. Willow House always strives to assist our clients to reach their optimal health (both physical & mental) by facilitating an environment that is safe, harmonious and client centered.
